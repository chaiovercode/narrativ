# Visual Styles

Narrativ offers multiple ways to style your visual content.

## Predefined Styles

### Cinematic
- Film-like quality with dramatic lighting
- Deep shadows and highlights
- Professional, polished look
- Best for: News, serious topics, professional content

### Vintage
- Retro aesthetic with warm tones
- Film grain texture
- Nostalgic feeling
- Best for: Historical topics, lifestyle, travel memories

### Cyberpunk
- Neon colors (pink, cyan, purple)
- Futuristic, tech-forward
- High contrast, dramatic
- Best for: Technology, gaming, future-focused content

### Minimalist
- Clean, simple compositions
- Limited color palette
- Focus on content
- Best for: Business, educational, quotes

### Watercolor
- Artistic, painted appearance
- Soft, flowing colors
- Organic feeling
- Best for: Art, nature, creative topics

## Custom Styles

### Creating a Custom Style

1. Go to **Create** page
2. Click **Create Custom Style**
3. Configure each aspect:

#### Art Style
The overall artistic approach:
- Photorealistic
- Illustration
- 3D render
- Painting
- Digital art

#### Color Palette
The dominant colors:
- Warm (reds, oranges, yellows)
- Cool (blues, greens, purples)
- Monochrome
- Vibrant
- Pastel
- Specific colors (e.g., "navy blue and gold")

#### Lighting
How light appears:
- Natural daylight
- Golden hour
- Studio lighting
- Dramatic shadows
- Soft, diffused
- Neon glow

#### Texture
Surface quality:
- Smooth
- Grainy (film grain)
- Rough
- Glossy
- Matte

#### Typography Style
Text appearance (for overlays):
- Bold sans-serif
- Elegant serif
- Handwritten
- Minimalist
- Retro

#### Background Style
What appears behind subjects:
- Solid color
- Gradient
- Abstract patterns
- Environmental/contextual
- Blurred/bokeh

### Saving Custom Styles

After configuring, click **Save Style** to:
- Name your style
- Add to your style library
- Reuse in future projects

### Style Extraction

Have an image whose style you love? Extract it:

1. Click **Extract Style**
2. Upload a reference image
3. AI analyzes and creates a style definition
4. Save and apply to your projects

## Style Tips

### Matching Style to Content

| Content Type | Recommended Styles |
|-------------|-------------------|
| News/Facts | Cinematic, Minimalist |
| Travel | Watercolor, Vintage |
| Technology | Cyberpunk, Minimalist |
| History | Vintage, Cinematic |
| Lifestyle | Watercolor, Vintage |
| Business | Minimalist, Cinematic |

### Consistency

For a cohesive story:
- Use the same style for all slides
- Maintain consistent color palette
- Keep lighting similar across images

### Experimentation

Don't be afraid to try unusual combinations:
- Historical topic + Cyberpunk style = Interesting contrast
- Tech topic + Watercolor = Unique, memorable

## Managing Styles

### View Your Styles

All saved styles appear in the style selector dropdown.

### Delete a Style

1. Open style selector
2. Find your custom style
3. Click the delete icon
4. Confirm deletion

Note: Predefined styles cannot be deleted.
